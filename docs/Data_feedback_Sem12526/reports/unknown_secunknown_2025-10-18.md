## What is Task Analysis?

**Task analysis** in educational planning means breaking down a larger goal (using an AI photo generation app) into manageable, teachable steps and skills. It helps you map out exactly **what the elderly learners need to do, know, or feel** at each stage of your session. This process is crucial for designing activities that are accessible, clear, and tailored to your audience‚Äôs needs.

---

### Why Perform Task Analysis?

- It allows you to **identify specific skills** (like opening an app, choosing a prompt, saving a photo) rather than assuming ‚Äúusing the AI app‚Äù is one simple task.
- It highlights **potential barriers** (technical terms, confusing steps, small buttons) and lets you plan for support or scaffolding.
- It connects learning objectives to hands-on actions‚Äîmaking your session measurable and adaptable.
- It ensures the session addresses **well-being or confidence goals**, not just technical skills (so elders leave feeling proud, less intimidated by technology).

---

### 2. Break the Goal Into Discrete Sub-Tasks

   - Turn on device  
   - Locate & open the AI photo app  
2. **Navigating the app interface:**  
   - Identify main buttons/icons

3. **Inputting prompts or selections:**  
   - Choose subject or style

4. **Viewing the generated image:**
