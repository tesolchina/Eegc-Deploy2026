What a topic sentence is
- A topic sentence is usually the first sentence of a paragraph.  
- It tells the reader the main idea that the paragraph will explain or prove.

- Directly linked to the thesis (uses the same idea or vocabulary).  
- Arguable or analytical (not just a fact).
